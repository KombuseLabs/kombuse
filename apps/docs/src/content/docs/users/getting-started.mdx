Welcome to Kombuse! This guide will help you get up and running.

## Installation

Download the latest release for your platform from the [releases page](https://github.com/kombuse/kombuse-mono/releases).

## First Steps

Once installed, launch the app and you're ready to go.

## Tickets

The tickets page is where you manage all your work items. Create tickets to track bugs, features, and tasks across your projects.
